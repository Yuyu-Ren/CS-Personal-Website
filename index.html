Free Printing in Burnside!

Are you in science? Or are pursuing a degree in arts and science? Then you qualify for up to 1000 pages of free printing every semester in Burnside basement. Just head over to the computer labs in 1B16 or 1B17 to gain access to different programs related to school and graphics design. Services courtesy of the Science Computer TaskForce.

Free Peripherals!

Come visit Reboot McGill in Ferrier 218 by the McConnell Engineering Building if you need a replacement mouse, keyboard, computer or monitor. We also have various cables and plugs. If you need something, it never hurts to ask! You can visit us on Facebook or in person at our office. Additionally, clicking on the logo will bring you to our official site.
